Package “A”

This package consists of a total of twenty meals (5 different entrees with one side each) serving a family of four for a week or for two adults for a two-week period. All meals are prepared, packaged and left in the fridge so the client can decide which to freeze and which to leave cold. Meals can be packaged in servings of one, two or four. This package is popular with clients who want to enjoy the service of a personal chef every two weeks, but do not want to commit to a weekly plan.

Package “B”

This package consists of a total of ten meals (5 different entrees with one side each) serving two people for a week. This package is popular with clients who don't want to cook or go out every night, but do want to eat at home five nights a week on a regular basis. This option is also ideal for clients with specific nutritional requirements who want to maintain an ongoing, organized dieting program. Individual packaging entails a small extra charge per serving.

Package “C”

This package consists of a total of six meals (3 different entrees with one side) serving two people during the week. This package is popular with clients who don't have time to cook and still enjoy eating out occasionally; they will also enjoy great food when they choose to eat in.
