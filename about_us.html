Continental Cuisine is about… You!

Your introduction to a world of more time and better living begins with an interview to assess your dietary priorities, your tastes and your dietary goals. Together, we'll determine what you want for dinner and the way you want your dinners prepared and packaged. On our agreed-upon cooking date I'll shop for fresh groceries, bring them along with my cooking equipment to your kitchen and prepare your meals on-site. I'll leave your kitchen spotless and filled with the delicious aromas of fine home cooking.

You will find that your meals are both tasty and reasonably priced. You'll save time and money; no more wasted time in supermarket check-out lines, fast food or supermarket frozen food with ingredients you've never heard of! And if dining out is an option, you limit the time you waste finding a place to park, waiting to be seated and served, and tipping your server as you leave. Now imagine you and your family together at the dinner table, enjoying delicious, healthy home-cooked meals prepared to your specifications — at your convenience!
